## Prompt 1

**Input:**
Summarize the importance of Artificial Intelligence in education.

**Output:**
Artificial Intelligence (AI) improves education by providing personalized learning, instant feedback, and easy access to educational resources. It helps students learn more effectively and supports teachers in managing classrooms.

## Prompt 2

**Input:**
Write a short paragraph on climate change.

**Output:**
Climate change affects weather patterns, ecosystems, and human life. It is mainly caused by pollution and greenhouse gas emissions. Using renewable energy, planting trees, and reducing pollution can help protect the environment.
